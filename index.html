HOW IT WORKS

From your voice to a finished action.

01

Say the word

"Hey Jarvis" wakes the voice loop, or just type into the chat window — both paths lead to the same brain.

02

It understands

An LLM reads what you said and decides: is this a fact to remember, a command to run, or just a conversation?

03

It acts

The right skill runs, the result is spoken or shown, and the exchange is remembered for next time.
